Ecosystem Glossary

AA (Account Aggregator): A consent-based financial data sharing network that lets lenders securely pull bank statements directly from the borrower's bank.
ULI (Unified Lending Interface): An RBI initiative that provides a single interface for lenders to fetch multi-source alternate data (GST, land records, utilities) for instant risk analysis.
OCEN (Open Credit Enablement Network): A protocol standardizing how digital apps/marketplaces integrate loans, enabling automated credit discovery, contracting, and disbursals.
Alternative Data: Digital footprints like GST compliance, daily UPI sales, EPFO payroll registries, and electricity consumption to underwrite collateral-free loans.
